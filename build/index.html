Ten-Second Taxi

You are the taxi driver in a town full of incredibly impatient people. Everyone wants to get to their destination in under ten seconds, and will jump out of your taxi if you take too long!

Controls should be self-explanatory - just drive.

I ran out of time to do auto-reverse, power-ups, opposition or traffic cars, more commentary, cash rewards for faster driving (it's currently just based on distance), nitro, handbrake turn, a menu system, a decent intro web page, etc. Next time, maybe! :)

I also did this with very little Internet access - only mobile phone browsing That was a challenge.

Cheers to the makers of Autotracker and SFXR in particular. I also used GIMP, Emscripten, QtCreator, C++, Python, etc.
