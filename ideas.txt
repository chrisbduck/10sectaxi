Ten-Second Taxi
---------------

- Driving game, vaguely crazy-taxi-like
- Pick up passengers automatically (as long as at not *very* high speeds)
- Passengers all want to get somewhere within ten seconds
  - Use silly excuses when needed :)

Controls:
- Left, right, accelerate, brake
- Handbrake turn
- Nitro

Power-ups:
- Hover-taxi - drive over fences and maybe small buildings
- Boat taxi - drive over water
- Turbo boost - Knight-Rider-style single jump over stuff
- Instant reverse - Nonsensical stop and turn around
